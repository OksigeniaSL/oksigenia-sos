Oksigenia SOS is a safety support tool for outdoor activities (cycling, hiking, sailing).

Key features:
- Smart impact and fall detection.
- Inactivity monitor (Dead Man's Switch).
- Automatic SMS alerts with GPS location to your trusted contact.
- Full privacy: works locally with no trackers.

IMPORTANT: This app is a support tool and DOES NOT replace professional emergency services. Operation depends on mobile coverage and GPS signal.
