v4.2.1 — Google-free

- Removed the Google Play Services location dependency that the geolocator plugin pulled in. The app now uses Android's own on-device location everywhere, so it is genuinely Google-free, a little smaller, and your location never passes through Google services.
- No functional change on de-Googled phones (they already used the native path). Outdoors, location behaves the same.
- Same signing key as before, so this updates cleanly over any previous install.
