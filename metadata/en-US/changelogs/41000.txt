Smart Beacon: post-SOS automatic position-update protocol. After an SOS is sent, if the user moves more than 300 m from the last known reference, an update SMS is sent with the new coordinates. Up to 20 updates over a 4-hour window, throttled to one SMS every 5 minutes. Battery cost while stationary is essentially zero (no SMS sent until movement is detected). Stops automatically when the user taps "Restart system".

Equitation profile: new activity profile for horse riding. Impact detection is disabled (horse cadence interferes with the human-walk detector). The Inactivity Monitor and manual SOS protect during the ride.

Three new languages: Polish (pl), Russian (ru), Norwegian Bokmål (nb). Brings the total to 11 supported languages.

Beacon parameters were calibrated for real outdoor rescue scenarios where rescuers may take 2–4 hours to arrive and the victim may wander, flee or be carried during that time.
