Smart Sentinel v4 is now feature-complete. v4.0.0 closes the multi-sport story with three additions on top of the v3.9.7 algorithm and activity profiles.

Catastrophic impact path: a high-G threshold per profile (10G Trekking, 13G Trail/MTB) fires the alarm immediately, skipping the 60-second observation window. The 30-second pre-alert on the alarm screen is still the user's chance to cancel.

GPS interval per profile: each profile declares its own sampling rate. Trekking and Mountaineering relax to 30 s / 30 m for battery savings. Paragliding tightens to 2 s / 5 m for fast 3D position updates. Trail/MTB and Professional stay at 5 s / 10 m.

Home screen widgets: a 1×1 panic SOS button and a 2×2 status widget showing active profile, sentinel state (green/yellow/orange/red), active modes and battery. Native Kotlin, no new dependencies, no new permissions.

Resolves Issue #3 (paragliding false positives): the Paragliding profile disables impact detection entirely.
