v4.2.4 — Battery fix

- Major battery fix: when monitoring is turned off, the background service now fully stops and releases its wakelock instead of keeping the device awake for nothing. Previously, with detection disabled, it could quietly drain the battery — hours of charge lost outdoors with no protection in return. After a reboot it now revives by itself only if monitoring was actually on.
- The persistent service notification no longer beeps on every action; it stays silent as it should.
- Removed the home-screen panic widget (rarely used, and its background polling was part of the drain).
- Validated on device. Same signing key, so this updates cleanly over any previous install.
